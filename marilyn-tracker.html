About the Marilyns

UK hills with at least 150m of topographic prominence on all sides, regardless of height. The name is a playful counterpart to Munros — named after Marilyn Monroe. Covering the whole of Britain and Ireland.

⚡ Auto-detected from Strava

Connect your Strava account once and Summit scans your entire GPS history — detecting every Marilyn you've passed within 150m of the summit coordinates. No manual entry for past walks.

📝 Rich ascent logs

Log companions, conditions and personal notes per ascent. Multiple ascents of the same hill tracked separately — your second round of a summit is its own day in your logbook.

🗺 Interactive topo map

Your bagged hills on a real topographic map. Zoom in to see contour lines and footpaths. Filter by region or completion status. See your progress at a glance.

🏆 UK Leaderboard

See how your Marilyns total compares to other hillwalkers across the UK. Follow friends, track rivals, celebrate completions together.
