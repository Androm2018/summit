Why Summit

Everything asked for.
Nothing missing.

Every feature Munro Bagger users requested — notes, companions, multiple rounds, area search — and automatic Strava detection nobody else has built.

⚡

Strava Auto-Detection

Connect once. Summit scans every GPS track you've ever recorded and automatically marks each summit you passed within 100m. Five years of hillwalking, populated in seconds.

📝

Rich Ascent Logs

Notes, companions, conditions, photos — per ascent, not per hill. Log your second round separately. Every detail of every day in the hills, preserved properly.

🔍

Search by Area

Filter by region, height, remoteness, season, or stalking closure. Find every Munro in Torridon, or every hill you climbed in winter. Not just by name.

🏴󠁧󠁢󠁳󠁣󠁴󠁿

Gaelic Pronunciations

Every hill name with correct Gaelic pronunciation — IPA notation and a phonetic guide. Know how to say "A' Bhuidheanach Bheag" before someone asks at the compleation dinner.

👥

Live Conditions

Community conditions reports — snow cover, path state, river crossings, midges. Real intelligence from people on the hill today, not last summer.

⬇

Export Anywhere

Download your full log as CSV or in formats compatible with hill-bagging.co.uk and Hill Lists. Your data stays yours.
